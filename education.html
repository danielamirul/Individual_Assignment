My Education Background

When I was 5 years old, I went to PASTI at my home and I studied there for 2 years. It was my first education institution that I was involved. At here is where I learned basic early education.

After that, when I am 7 years old I went to SK Kempadang as my primary school until I reached 12 years old. I took my UPSR examination there and got 3A2B for the result.

For my secondary school, I choose to studied at SMK Tanjong Lumpur which is a very nice school here at my place. I studied here for 5 years and have taken 2 important examination PT3 and SPM. Alhamdulillah I get a good result on both of the examination.

When I have finished my high school, I apply UPU to countinue my studies in higher level and I got to study at UiTM Raub for Banking Studies course. I studied here for 5 semester and finished at early year 2020.

Later, I apply UPU for bachelor and I got offer to UiTM Kota Bharu for Marketing course. I accept it and here I am, stuying at UiTM Kota Bharu.
